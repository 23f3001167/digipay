DigiPay

Transform your school into a cashless campus with secure, instant digital payments for students, parents, and shopkeepers.

How It Works

1
Parent Adds Funds

Parents securely add money to their child's digital wallet through the parent dashboard.

2
Student Makes Purchases

Students use QR codes or student ID to make purchases at campus shops.

3
Shopkeeper Receives Payment

Shopkeepers instantly receive digital payments into their wallet after each transaction.

4
Admin Settlement

School admin settles shopkeeper balances and maintains system oversight.

Key Features

Campus Digital Wallet

Secure instant digital payments throughout the school campus without physical cash.

School Admin Control

Complete administrative control over users, balances, transactions, and shop management.

Parent Monitoring

Parents can recharge wallets and monitor spending patterns from anywhere, anytime.

Shop Integration

Seamless integration with campus shops for digital sales tracking and settlements.

QR Code Payments

Quick and secure transactions using QR code scanning technology.

Real-time Tracking

Live transaction tracking and instant balance updates for all users.
